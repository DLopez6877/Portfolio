Background

I am from Vancouver, Washington. I recently left my job as an in-home Installation and Repair Technician for Comcast. Before that I worked in the printing Industry as DocuMart. During that time, I served part of my 8 years in the Washington Army National Guard where I was a Signal Support Systems Specialist working in the field as part of a field artillery unit. The first half of my service I was also attending college classes at Clark College.

Why I started class at Epicodus

I started class with little to no experience in coding. Every job I have ever held had been in some sort of field that would require me to be on my feet all day and far away from computers. However, I have always been passionate about computers and always wanted to learn as much as I could about them. Over the years I had dabbled with coding during my freetime but never much more than just edditing a MySpace page or learning a tid bit here and there from a friend. One day my roommate jokingly asked me if I wanted to quit my job and go to a coding bootcamp with him. At first I jokingly laughed right back and said, "yea right" but after a while I thought about it more and realised that's exactly what I want to do. I saved up money for months, applied to Epicodus, and here I am!
